Privacy Policy

Welcome to Zen Tea. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This brief privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect
When you register an account, place an order, or interact with our services, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, delivery address, and payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information
-We use the information we collect solely to:

-Process, track, and fulfill your beverage orders.

-Manage your account and shopping cart preferences.

--Communicate with you regarding order updates or customer support.

Improve our system and user experience.

3. Data Protection and Sharing
We implement standard security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share necessary details with trusted partners (such as payment gateways or delivery services) strictly for the purpose of fulfilling your order.

4. Cookies
Our website uses cookies to keep you logged in, remember your cart items, and provide a seamless browsing experience.

5. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at zentea@email.com.my.
